---
title: "Get Single Order"
api: "GET https://api.getscale.ng/api/v1/hub/shop/{shopId}/order/{orderId}"
---

Retrieves full order details, including ordered products, customer details, and shipping address.



### Authorizations

<ParamField header="X-Shop-API-Key" type="string" required>
  Your Secret API Key (`sk_live_...` or `sk_test_...`) generated from the Developer dashboard. Must be kept secret.
</ParamField>


### Path Parameters

<ParamField path="shopId" type="string" required>
  The unique UUID of the shop.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="orderId" type="number" required>
  The unique ID of the order.
</ParamField>
