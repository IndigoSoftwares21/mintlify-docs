---
title: "Update Customer"
api: "PUT https://api.sabivendor.com/api/v1/hub/shop/{shopId}/customers/{customerId}"
---

Updates customer details (e.g. shipping address or contact info).



### Authorizations

<ParamField header="X-Shop-API-Key" type="string" required>
  Your Secret API Key (`sk_live_...` or `sk_test_...`) generated from the Developer dashboard. Must be kept secret.
</ParamField>


### Path Parameters

<ParamField path="shopId" type="string" required>
  The unique UUID of the shop.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="customerId" type="string" required>
  The unique UUID of the customer.
</ParamField>

### Request Body (JSON)

<ParamField body="address" type="string">
  Updated delivery address.
</ParamField>
