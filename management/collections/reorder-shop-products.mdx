---
title: "Reorder Shop Products"
api: "PUT https://api.getscale.ng/api/v1/hub/shop/{shopId}/products/reorder"
---

Configures display sorting orders on general product listings catalog.



### Authorizations

<ParamField header="X-Shop-API-Key" type="string" required>
  Your Secret API Key (`sk_live_...` or `sk_test_...`) generated from the Developer dashboard. Must be kept secret.
</ParamField>


### Path Parameters

<ParamField path="shopId" type="string" required>
  The unique UUID of the shop.
</ParamField>

### Request Body (JSON)

<ParamField body="orders" type="object[]" required>
  General shop product sorting configurations.
  <Expandable title="properties">
    <ParamField body="productId" type="number" required>
      Product catalog ID.
    </ParamField>
    <ParamField body="sortOrder" type="number" required>
      Display index offset.
    </ParamField>
  </Expandable>
</ParamField>
